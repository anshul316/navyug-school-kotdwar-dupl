Infrastructure & Facilities

A Campus That Inspires Learning, Creativity,

and Growth

At Navyug Senior Secondary Public School, Kotdwar, our campus is more than just a collection of buildings — it's a living space that nurtures curiosity, imagination, and lifelong learning.

Academic Blocks

Our classrooms are spacious, bright, and well-ventilated, providing a comfortable environment for focused learning. We follow a traditional teaching setup that encourages close interaction between teachers and students.

To make learning more engaging, students also attend sessions in our Digital Learning Hall, where lessons are supported by audio-visual tools and interactive presentations.

Science and Computer Laboratories

Hands-on learning is at the heart of our academic philosophy. The school houses well-equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer Science, enabling students to explore, experiment, and understand concepts practically.

Each lab meets all CBSE safety and quality standards, supervised by trained educators who make learning both engaging and safe.

Green & Sustainable Campus

In line with our values, Navyug promotes sustainability and environmental care. The campus features tree-lined walkways, rainwater harvesting systems, and a mini nursery where students learn the joy of growing plants. Waste segregation, clean energy initiatives, and eco-clubs reinforce our students’ role as responsible citizens of tomorrow.

Safety & Security

Student safety is our top priority. The school campus is under 24×7 CCTV surveillance, with security staff and a clearly defined safety protocol. Fire safety measures, emergency exits, and periodic safety drills ensure that every child learns in a secure, caring environment.

Transport Facility

Transport Facility Our fleet of well-maintained buses covers major areas of Kotdwar and nearby regions, each equipped with GPS tracking, CCTV, and trained attendants to ensure student safety and punctuality.

Library — A World of Wisdom

The heart of our academic life, the library, houses an extensive collection of books across literature, science, technology, and more. A peaceful and inspiring space, it encourages students to explore, reflect, and cultivate the lifelong habit of reading.

The Navyug Advantage

The Navyug Advantage At Navyug Senior Secondary Public School, every facility — from labs and classrooms to sports fields and creative studios — reflects our belief that education is not confined to walls, but flourishes in spaces that inspire. We don’t just build infrastructure; we build experiences that prepare children to think, create, and lead with purpose.

Sports & Physical Education

We believe that fitness builds confidence and discipline. The school offers excellent sports facilities including:

  • A spacious playground for football, cricket, and athletics
  • Basketball, volleyball, and badminton courts
  • Indoor games zone for, chess, and carrom
  • Yoga and Taekwondo sessions for focus and self-defence

Regular inter-house tournaments, fitness drills, and sports meets promote teamwork, perseverance, and sportsmanship among students.
