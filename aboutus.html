About School

Navyug Senior Secondary Public School

Naya Yug — A New Era of Learning

Every morning at Navyug feels like a fresh start. “Naya Yug” isn’t just our name — it’s our promise: every child gets a new chance to wonder, try, fall, learn, and begin again.

We believe childhood should be full of small discoveries and brave experiments, for it’s through these everyday moments that children grow into curious thinkers and confident learners.



Our Journey

Our story began in 1999 with one classroom and a big dream — to create a school that prepares children for new beginnings. Founded by the Late Mrs. Shaily Negi and Mr. Hukam Singh Negi, Navyug has grown over the years into a thriving, close-knit family that has nurtured generations of learners.

With more than 25 years of commitment to quality education, our journey continues to be guided by the belief that when a child is given a strong foundation, there are no limits to what they can achieve.
