NAVYUG SCHOOL

Founder & Chairman

Late Mrs. Shaily Negi

Founder & Visionary Educator

For Late Mrs. Shaily Negi, Navyug was never just a school — it was her second home, her life’s purpose, and her greatest joy.

Every child who entered Navyug was, to her, her own. She believed that true education is not limited to books or classrooms — it lives in the warmth of care, the guidance of love, and the strength of values.

She poured her heart into this institution, nurturing every student with the affection of a mother and the wisdom of a teacher. Her compassion was boundless — she celebrated each child’s success as her own and stood beside them in their struggles. Those who knew her say that she didn’t just run a school; she built a family.

Even today, former students walk back through these gates — not just to revisit their memories, but to feel her presence, to seek her blessings, and to thank the woman who shaped their lives. Her spirit lives in every classroom, every smile, every achievement that carries the name Navyug.

Her legacy continues to guide us, reminding us that education, at its heart, is an act of love. Her blessings will forever remain with every student who becomes a part of the Navyug family.
