NAVYUG SCHOOL

Founder & Chairman

Mr. Hukam Singh Negi

Founder & Chairman

A visionary leader with a deep belief in the power of education, Mr. Hukam Singh Negi is the guiding force behind Navyug's enduring strength and values.

He built this institution with a dream — to make quality education accessible to every child in Kotdwar and to create a place where discipline, learning, and humanity walk hand in hand.

Under his leadership, Navyug has flourished as a school that blends tradition with progress — a school where students are taught not only how to succeed, but how to live with integrity and humility. His unwavering dedication and quiet strength continue to shape the school's growth, inspiring teachers, students, and parents alike.

For him, Navyug is not merely an institution — it is a living testament to the shared dream he began with Late Mrs. Shaily Negi. Together, they sowed the seeds of a new era in education, and those roots continue to nurture generations of learners.
