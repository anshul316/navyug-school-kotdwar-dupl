NAVYUG POLICY

School Policies

At Navyug Senior Secondary Public School, our policies are designed to ensure a safe, disciplined, and nurturing environment where every student can grow academically, socially, and emotionally. These guidelines reflect our core values — respect, responsibility, and integrity.

NAVYUG SCHOOL

Parent–Teacher Interaction

We believe that a child's success depends on strong collaboration between home and school. Parent-Teacher Interaction Meetings (PTMs) are held regularly to discuss each child's academic progress, behavior, and emotional well-being.

NAVYUG SCHOOL

Award Policy

Annual Awards honour students for outstanding performance, leadership, and community service.
Special recognition is also given for good conduct, creativity, and contribution to school life.
Certificates of Excellence and Certificates of Participation are awarded for achievements in academics, sports, and co-curricular activities.
NAVYUG SCHOOL

School Rules & Discipline

Discipline forms the backbone of character education at Navyug. We expect our students to uphold the highest standards of respect and personal responsibility.

  • ● Students must behave courteously and uphold the dignity of the school at all times.
  • ● Disrespectful, unruly, or indecent behavior will invite disciplinary action.
  • ● School property must be respected. Any damage will be charged to the student responsible.
NAVYUG SCHOOL

Use of Unfair Means

Integrity is a core value at Navyug. The use of unfair means in examinations or assignments is considered a serious violation of ethics and will result in cancellation of results and further disciplinary action, as per CBSE guidelines.

Leave Policy
  • » Leave may be granted only for unavoidable reasons and must be applied for in advance.
  • » Leave on medical grounds exceeding two days must be supported by a medical certificate.
  • » Absence during tests/exams without prior approval will lead to zero grade.
  • » Absence without information for more than 30 consecutive days will result in the student's name being struck off the rolls.
Meeting the Teachers or Principal
  • » Parents must request appointments in advance.
  • » Entry into classrooms during school hours is not permitted.
Digital & Social Conduct

Students are expected to use digital tools responsibly. Cyberbullying, sharing inappropriate content, or disrespectful behavior towards peers or staff will be treated as a disciplinary offence.

Environmental Responsibility (added for holistic education)

Students are encouraged to be stewards of nature. Activities that promote cleanliness, waste reduction, and respect for the environment are integral to our school culture.

NAVYUG SCHOOL

The Navyug Code of Conduct

At Navyug Senior Secondary Public School, we follow a simple rule:

"Be respectful. Be responsible. Be kind."

These values guide every policy and decision, ensuring that our school remains a safe, inclusive, and joyful space for all.
