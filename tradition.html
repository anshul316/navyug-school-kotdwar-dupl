Traditions at Navyug

Celebrating Moments, Memories, and Milestones Together

At Navyug Senior Secondary Public School, traditions are the heartbeat of our school life. They connect our past with our present and remind every student that learning is also about laughter, celebration, and togetherness.

Investiture Ceremony

A proud moment when our young leaders take their oath of responsibility. The ceremony marks the beginning of a new leadership journey filled with confidence, humility, and teamwork.

Summer Camp

A blend of fun, learning, and adventure. Our summer camp offers students opportunities to explore hobbies, learn new skills, and enjoy outdoor activities under the guidance of teachers and mentors.

Farewell Ceremony

Saying goodbye is never easy, but our farewell ceremonies are filled with gratitude and best wishes for the future. It's a time to celebrate the journey our seniors have completed.
