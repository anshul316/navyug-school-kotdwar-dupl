Admission Process



  • Inquiry & Visit – Parents are encouraged to visit the school, meet our staff, and explore the campus.
  • Application Form – Collect and submit the admission form along with required documents.
  • Interaction/Assessment – Depending on the class, students may undergo a short interaction or assessment to understand their learning level.
  • Confirmation of Admission – Once selected, parents are guided through the fee submission and enrollment process.

Our admission team is always available to assist parents and ensure a smooth experience. We look forward to welcoming you into our family, where your child will receive not just education, but an environment to truly learn, grow, and thrive.
